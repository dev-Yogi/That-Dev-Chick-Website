About me

I am a creative Jr. Web Developer, Jr. Web Designer, programming curriculum developer & Web Development Instructor.

I graduated with a degree in Web Development and have been building websites as well as a variety of different college level programming and web development curriculum since then.

My focus is on creating engaging, accessible & performant interfaces for humans.

Teaching web development is also a passion of mine. I have worked at various high schools in the metro area and continue to instruct accelerated college level courses at AIM Code School.
